Shipping Information

We offer free standard shipping to Canada when you spend $50, and a flat rate price of $9.75 for orders less than $50.


SHIPPING OUTSIDE OF CANADA

We do not currently ship internationally.


DELIVERY TIMES

Please allow at least 2 business days for your order to be processed.

Note: Orders will not be processed on Friday June 24th and Friday July 1st. We do not process orders on Canadian statutory holidays or Quebec provincial holidays, so that our employees can spend time with their families.


CANADA STANDARD DELIVERY TIMES: 3-8 Business Days


HOW DO I CHANGE MY ORDER?

WHAT DO I DO IF THE ORDER I RECEIVED IS WRONG/DAMAGED?
