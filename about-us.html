Growth and Produce.

All of our tea is naturally grown with biodynamic farm techniques and handpicked. Our ingredients of fruit teas are made with organic fruits which are produced by local farmers. Tealeaves wastes are used to produce accessories such as tea packaging.

Accessories.

All of our packaging and accessories (cups, tags, tea bags, etc.) are made from recycling materials (paper, fiber, plastics, etc.) and is biodegradable. The packagings for teas which need to be transported from other places are light-weighted, which contributes to energy saving.

Events.

We have been actively participating environment related events and festivals. And we are organizing tea workshops, environment workshops, culture workshops and movie nights every now and then.

Desserts.

All of our desserts are homemake using organic and local ingredients, as well as tea leaves.
